Digital Attention Rescue stores all data locally on your computer. When you uninstall the extension, most data is automatically removed by Chrome.

What was stored locally:

  • Browsing activity patterns (last 7 days)
  • Site classifications you created
  • Your streak and achievement progress
  • Extension settings and preferences

ðŸ§¹ Manual Data Cleanup (Optional)

If you want to ensure all extension data is completely removed, you can manually clear Chrome's local storage:

  • Open Chrome DevTools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I)
  • Go to the Application tab
  • Under Storage, click Clear site data
  • Or type chrome://settings/siteData in the address bar and search for extension-related data
